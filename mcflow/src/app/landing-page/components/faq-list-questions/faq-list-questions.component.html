Frequently asked questions

How does MCflow work?

This site lets you earn MCF points by completing simple tasks with your PC or mobile phone.

 Who can be a member of MCFlow?

Generally, anyone can be a member of MCFlow as all you need is an internet enabled device to enable you to take tasks on our platform.

 How to register?

Open the registration form and fill in the blank fields, enter and create a password.

 What is the registration fee?

It depends on the package you are opting in for. On MCflow, there are four (4) unique registration pricing packages.

 How long does it take before i recieve my withdrawal?

Withdrawals are sent within an hour after requesting them.
